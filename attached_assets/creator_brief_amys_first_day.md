# Creator Brief: "Amy's First Day" — Episode 1 of *Stuff Amy Did Today*

## Overview

- **Product/Brand:** Done By Amy — AI automation for Australian small business
- **Series:** *Stuff Amy Did Today* (Episode 1 of 6)
- **Platform:** TikTok (primary), Instagram Reels & YouTube Shorts (cut-downs)
- **Master duration:** 22 seconds (TikTok cut: 18s, Reels cut: 28s with extra proof beat, Shorts cut: 30s with subscribe end-card)
- **Tone:** Dry, deadpan, self-deprecating Aussie tradie humour. UGC — feels like a real tradie filmed it on his lunch break, NOT an ad.
- **Target Audience:** Australian small-business owners — tradies, lawn care, NDIS providers, clinics, solar installers. Sole operators or 1–5 staff. Drowning in admin, missing calls, working past 9pm on quotes.
- **Goal:** Establish the "Amy" character + signature opener so viewers recognise the series on sight.

---

## The Signature Opener (USE THIS ON EVERY EPISODE)

Every episode in this series MUST start with the same 2-second beat. This is what makes the series recognisable:

1. iPhone *ding* sound effect (use the iOS default text tone)
2. Notification banner slides down from the top of screen
3. **Sender = "Amy"** in your brand purple `#A855F7`
4. Cut to the tradie's face — eye-roll, smirk, or shocked look

Treat it like a sitcom intro. After 3–4 posts your audience will start scrolling on reflex when they see it.

## Shot List (Master Cut — 22 seconds)

### Shot 1 — HOOK (0:00–0:02)

- **Camera:** Close-up, selfie-angle, phone held arm's length
- **What to film:** Tradie sitting in the driver's seat of his ute. Sceptical raised eyebrow. Holding his phone. Golden hour morning light.
- **On-screen text:** `"Just hired my first employee."`
- **Say this:** *(silent — let the face do the work)*
- **Wardrobe/Setting:** Hi-vis shirt or workwear, dashboard slightly cluttered, tradie ute interior
- **Mood:** Sceptical, "what have I done"

### Shot 2 — REVEAL (0:02–0:05)

- **Camera:** Close-up of the phone screen (POV, looking down at his own hand)
- **What to film:** Phone shows a single notification from "Amy". Calloused tradie hand visible.
- **On-screen text:** `"Her name's Amy. She's... not real."`
- **Say this:** *(silent — sound effect: iPhone notification ding)*
- **Mood:** Mystery, intrigue. The reveal of the "joke"

### Shot 3 — PROBLEM (0:05–0:09)

- **Camera:** Wide shot, low angle. Phone propped on the ground or filmed by a mate.
- **What to film:** Tradie halfway up an extension ladder cleaning a gutter, hands full of tools. The unattended iPhone sits on the open tailgate of his ute below, *visibly* buzzing with multiple notifications.
- **On-screen text:** `"Watch what happens when I miss calls."`
- **Say this:** *(silent — sound effect: phone buzzing repeatedly)*
- **Props:** Aluminium extension ladder, gutter cleaning tools, ute with tailgate down
- **Mood:** Helpless. The classic tradie problem.

### Shot 4 — DEMO (0:09–0:14)

- **Camera:** Screen recording of the phone (or close-up filmed over the shoulder)
- **What to film:** Three SMS conversations stacked, each showing a customer asking about a quote and "Amy" replying instantly in a purple chat bubble. A calendar booking pops up: `"Tuesday 9am — LOCKED IN"`. Speed it up 1.5×.
- **On-screen text:** `"Meanwhile, on her side..."` (top), then a counter ticking: `"Leads replied: 3 · Quote sent · Booking confirmed"`
- **Say this:** *(silent — sound effect: light whoosh per message, soft ka-ching on booking)*
- **Mood:** Speed, competence, productivity porn.

### Shot 5 — PUNCHLINE (0:14–0:18)

- **Camera:** Medium close-up of the tradie back at ground level
- **What to film:** Tradie standing next to his ute, holding the phone in front of his face. Jaw slightly dropped. Sweaty forehead, smudge of dirt on cheek (authenticity sells).
- **On-screen text:** `"She booked 3 jobs while I was up there."`
- **Say this:** "...mate."
- **Mood:** Genuine disbelief.

### Shot 6 — CTA / LOOP (0:18–0:22)

- **Camera:** Selfie-angle, sitting on the open tailgate of the ute
- **What to film:** Tradie cracks a can of Solo, takes a sip, deadpans direct to camera.
- **On-screen text:** `"Done By Amy — $99/month."` (small, bottom-third)
- **Say this:** "Day 1. I think I'm cooked."
- **Mood:** Resigned, defeated by his own success. Looks down at phone again as it dings (which loops back to Shot 1 of the next episode).

## A/B Variants — Film All Three Hooks

Same body, different openings. Test for 7 days each. Cheapest creative experiment you'll ever run.

1. **Hook A — Stop-Scrolling Callout:** *"Stop scrolling if you're a tradie who misses more calls than you answer."* (Direct, audience self-selects)
2. **Hook B — POV (current master):** *"Just hired my first employee."* (Silent text, mystery)
3. **Hook C — Number Hook:** *"I missed 14 calls this week. Here's what my $99 'employee' did about it."* (Specificity = credibility)

## Filming Notes

- [ ] Film on an iPhone — no DSLR, no ring light. UGC aesthetic converts 3–4× polished.
- [ ] Natural light only. Golden hour for hook/CTA, midday sun for the worksite shots.
- [ ] Film each shot as a separate clip — don't try to do it in one take. Modular footage = future-proof.
- [ ] Total filming time: ~90 minutes including setup.
- [ ] Wardrobe: real workwear. Hi-vis preferred, slightly dirty better than clean.
- [ ] Props: phone, extension ladder, ute with tailgate, gutter cleaning tools, can of Solo (or VB).
- [ ] **Cast a REAL tradie.** Not an actor. Comments will sniff out a fake in 2 seconds. Offer a customer $200 + free month of Amy.

## Audio

- **VO:** Spoken Aussie accent on Shot 5 + 6 only. Everything else is text + sound effects.
- **Trending sound:** Layer a current trending Aussie TikTok sound at 15% volume under the SFX. Refresh weekly — sounds have a 7–14 day shelf life. Check [TokChart Australia](https://tokchart.com/au) the morning you post.
- **SFX list:** iOS notification ding (Shots 1, 2, 6), phone buzz loop (Shot 3), message whoosh ×3 (Shot 4), light ka-ching on booking pop-up (Shot 4), can crack (Shot 6).

## Success Metrics (review at Day 7)

- 3-sec retention ≥ 65% → hook is working
- Watch-through ≥ 45% → body is working
