# Done By Amy — AI Chatbots Social Calendar (3 Months)

**Product focus:** AI Chatbots — *Answer every enquiry, instantly.* From $149/month.
**Platforms:** Instagram (feed) + Facebook.
**Posting cadence:** 3 posts/week (Mon · Wed · Fri) for 12 weeks = 36 posts.
**Goal:** Turn missed enquiries into booked jobs by showing Aussie SMBs what a 24/7 AI chatbot actually does.

---

## How to use this calendar

- Each post has a **primary caption** that works on both platforms, plus a short **FB note** where the platform differs.
- **Instagram:** hook lives in the first ~125 characters (before "…more"). 3–5 hashtags only — more reads as spam now.
- **Facebook:** drop hashtags to 1–2 (or none), and paste your link **directly in the caption** — FB shows clickable links natively, IG doesn't. On IG, send people to "link in bio."
- **Visuals:** keep the brand look — near-black background, electric purple `#A855F7` accent, big bold sans type, lots of breathing room. Portrait 4:5 (1080×1350) outperforms square for reach.
- Swap the example trades (sparky, plumber, salon, cafe, clinic) for whoever you're targeting that month.

---

## Content pillars (the rotation)

1. **Pain point** — the cost of a missed/slow enquiry.
2. **Feature spotlight** — 24/7, lead qualifying, booking, FAQ deflection.
3. **Myth-bust / education** — how it actually works, what it won't do.
4. **Real scenario** — a day-in-the-life for a specific trade.
5. **Objection handling** — "too dear," "too techy," "sounds robotic."
6. **Behind the scenes** — Amy's POV, build process, the "why."
7. **Engagement** — polls, questions, fill-in-the-blanks.
